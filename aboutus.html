About Us

Welcome to Food Sea
where passion for food meets a commitment to service. Nestled in the heart of Serampore, we invite you to experience culinary excellence in a warm and inviting atmosphere.

Our Story

Our story began with a vision - to create a haven where flavors dance on your palate, where every meal is an unforgettable journey. With a team of dedicated chefs, we curate each dish using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients, infusing creativity into every recipe. From sizzling appetizers to indulgent desserts, our menu is a celebration of diverse tastes and vibrant aromas.

Our Community

Beyond serving exquisite meals, we are committed to sustainability and community. We partner with local farmers and artisans to support the region's agriculture and reduce our environmental footprint. With each dish served, we aim to contribute to a healthier planet and a thriving community.

Join us at Food Sea and embark on a culinary adventure that delights the senses and nourishes the soul. From the first bite to the last, we promise an experience that lingers long after the meal ends.
